Demand4Restoration

Demand4Restoration is a research project that explores how integrating local, regional, and national restoration demands can improve the social and ecological effectiveness of restoration in Madagascar. By analyzing over 30 existing interventions and co-creating new ones with communities, the project generates practical insights to guide more equitable and sustainable restoration policies across the country.
